Technical Projects

  • Blind Spot Monitoring System:This project is about detecting other vehicles located to driver’s side and rear, And to warn driver about vehicle present in its blind spot. Driver gets visual and audible indication.
  • Wireless Bot Car Using Wi-Fi Module ESP8266 (IOT) (TE):This project was designed to participate in WRC robot race at the 5th th International Robotics League & World Robotics Championship. The bot was controlled via mobile using an application through Wi-Fi.
  • Drivetrain designing(formula student project):As a member of formula student team of our college Triumphant racer’s, A drivetrain was designed which delivers power from engine to wheels of formula student vehicle.
  • Humanoid robot designing:This project was developed under the startup company PNT solutions. This robot is aimed to use in public places (malls, public and social events) for the advertisement .It has 22inch display in the center of the body and complex mechanism of hands and neck.
  • Smart water quality monitoring system and control system(Final year project):This is the final year project, which is developed to sense parameters of water like PH, turbidity, total dissolved solvents, temperature. These parameters readings are uploaded on server, user can view these parameters remotely on mobile phone via Blynk app.
